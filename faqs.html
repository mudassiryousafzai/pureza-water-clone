Our FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our FAQs to find quick solutions to your common questions. We believe clean water should come with clear answers. Whether it's about delivery, quality, or pricing — we've got you covered.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver bottled water to all areas of Rawalpindi and Islamabad, covering both residential and commercial locations.

What sizes of water bottles are available?

We offer 19-liter bottles for home and office use, perfect for families and businesses. These bottles are designed for easy handling and maximum freshness.

Do you offer regular delivery schedules?

Yes, we offer flexible delivery schedules including daily, weekly, and custom plans to suit your needs. You can easily modify your schedule through our customer service.

Is your water safe to drink?

Our water goes through a rigorous purification process including reverse osmosis, UV sterilization, and mineral balancing to ensure the highest safety standards.

What are your delivery charges?

We offer free delivery in most areas of Rawalpindi and Islamabad. For specific locations or bulk orders, please contact us for detailed pricing information.

Do I need to return the empty bottles?

Yes, we operate on a bottle exchange system. When we deliver fresh water, we collect the empty bottles for cleaning and refilling, promoting environmental sustainability.

Can I cancel or reschedule my delivery?

Yes, you can easily cancel or reschedule your delivery by calling us or sending a WhatsApp message. We recommend giving us at least 24 hours notice for changes.
